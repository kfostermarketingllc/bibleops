## üö´ Brand Don'ts

**Visual:**
- Don't use purple, pink, or red as primary colors
- Don't use decorative or script fonts
- Don't overcrowd layouts
- Don't use low-quality images

**Copy:**
- Don't oversell or exaggerate
- Don't use religious jargon excessively
- Don't make theological claims
- Don't disparage traditional methods
- Don't promise perfection

**Tone:**
- Don't be overly casual or chatty
- Don't be academic or stuffy
- Don't be pushy or aggressive
- Don't be vague about capabilities
